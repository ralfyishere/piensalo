# Near-miss example — skill must NOT fire

**Task:** "Quote the vendor's published pricing tiers verbatim in the comparison doc: $29/mo Starter, $99/mo Pro, $499/mo Enterprise, per their pricing page."

**Why it must not fire:** This matches a counterindication: every figure is a labeled quotation traceable verbatim to a source (the vendor's pricing page). Nothing was derived, so there is nothing to recompute.

**Why firing would hurt:** "Recomputing" quoted list prices is meaningless work — there are no inputs to rederive them from — and second-guessing verbatim quotations risks corrupting the one thing the task requires: fidelity to the source.
