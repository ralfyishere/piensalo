# BENCHMARK — rederive-the-numbers

## Evidence ladder
Every Piénsalo micro-skill carries exactly one status from this ladder:

- **DESIGNED** — specified from documented reasoning-failure modes; not yet executed as a packaged skill.
- **SMOKE_TESTED** — executed end-to-end in live sessions and behaves as specified; no measured lift is claimed.
- **EXPERIMENTALLY_TESTED** — showed measurable lift over baseline in at least one controlled comparison.
- **REPLICATED** — the measured lift reproduced in an independent run or setting.
- **PROMOTED** — replicated and adopted as a default-on component.
- **NARROW** — a real measured effect, but confined to a narrow task class; not claimed beyond it.
- **REJECTED** — tested and shown not to help (or to hurt); retained for the record.

## Current status: EXPERIMENTALLY_TESTED

Micro-skill repair content lifted a weak model on procedural tasks in controlled runs; the effect is task-concentrated, and automatic router selection of micro-skills is a separate, unproven layer.
