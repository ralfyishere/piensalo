# Near-miss example (must NOT fire)

**Task:** "Jot down rough notes on the three pricing options for me — just so I don't forget the tradeoffs."

**Why it must not fire:** the requester is the sole consumer (counterindication: no downstream reader). There is no forwarded audience, no register or confidentiality constraint, and the requester can ask follow-ups directly.

**Why firing would hurt:** polishing private working notes for an imagined audience adds tokens and formality the reader never asked for, and may strip the shorthand and technical detail that make the notes useful to their only reader.
