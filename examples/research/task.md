# Task

You are handed two internal briefs about the same market and asked: "How big
is the addressable market for our warehouse-robotics retrofit kit? One
number, please, for the board deck."

- **Brief A (sales team, March):** "Industry analysts size the retrofit
  segment at **$4.2B** globally, growing 22% a year."
- **Brief B (finance team, May):** "Re-based to facilities that can
  physically accept a retrofit (ceiling height, power, union rules), the
  serviceable segment is **$2.9B**; the $4.2B figure counts greenfield
  automation spend that never buys retrofits."
