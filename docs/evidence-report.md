# Evidence Report (skeleton)

> **Status: SKELETON.** This report is the narrative companion to
> [EVIDENCE.md](../EVIDENCE.md), which is the source of truth for all
> capability claims and is maintained by the evidence workstream. Nothing in
> this file may state a number or level that EVIDENCE.md does not contain.

## Purpose

EVIDENCE.md is a ledger: claim → level → receipts. This report, once
written, adds the connective tissue a reader needs: what was tested, in what
order, what changed our minds, and how to audit us.
