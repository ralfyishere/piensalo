# Task: Checkout price after stacked discounts

A jacket lists at **$250.00**. At checkout, three reductions apply in
sequence, each computed on the running amount (not the list price):

- a 30% seasonal markdown;
- then a 20% clearance reduction on the marked-down price;
- then a 10% loyalty discount on the result.

Compute the final price paid. Deliver, in your answer file:

1. A short `WORKING:` section showing the price after each reduction.
2. A line exactly of the form `FINAL_PRICE: <amount, two decimals>`
   (leading $ optional).

The summary line is parsed by a script; get the format exact.
