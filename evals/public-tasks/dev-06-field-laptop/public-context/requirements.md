# Cascade Ridge Field Survey — Laptop Procurement Requirements (v2.1, binding)

Every requirement below is binding unless marked "(optional)". Requirements
in Section 5 are safety/operational hard limits: a candidate that fails any
one of them is disqualified outright, regardless of how well it scores on
everything else.

## 1. Performance
R-01. 8-core CPU or better.
R-02. 32 GB RAM or more.
R-03. Runs the GIS suite at 60 fps on a 4K basemap (discrete GPU expected).

## 2. Display
R-04. 15-inch or larger screen.
R-05 (optional). OLED panel.
R-06. Screen readable in direct sunlight (>= 800 nits).

## 3. Storage & connectivity
R-07. 1 TB SSD or larger.
R-08. Built-in cellular (5G) modem.

## 4. Commercial
R-09. Unit price at or under USD 3,000.
R-10 (optional). Trade-in credit for old units.

## 5. Field / safety hard limits (each is a disqualifier)
R-11. Must sustain a full 9-hour survey shift on a single battery charge
under GIS load. There is NO mains power and NO vehicle power at the survey
sites; a unit that cannot run 9 hours unplugged is disqualified outright.
R-12. Battery must be field-swappable (hot or cold) by the crew without
tools, OR the unit must ship with a second internal cell — because R-11 has
no fallback if a cell fails mid-shift.
R-13. Operating-temperature floor of -20 C or colder (surveys run into
early winter).
R-14. MIL-STD-810H drop and dust rating.
