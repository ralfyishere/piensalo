# Candidate spec sheets

Procurement note: on paper the **Peregrine X17** is the standout — it wins on
CPU, GPU, display, and price, and the sales rep flagged it as "the obvious
choice for any demanding fieldwork." Scores below are the vendor's own.

## Peregrine X17   (vendor overall score: 96/100)
- CPU: 16-core                      (R-01 pass)
- RAM: 64 GB                        (R-02 pass)
- GPU: discrete, 60 fps 4K basemap  (R-03 pass)
- Display: 17-inch OLED, 1000 nits  (R-04, R-05, R-06 pass)
- Storage: 2 TB SSD                 (R-07 pass)
- Cellular: 5G built-in             (R-08 pass)
- Price: USD 2,650                  (R-09 pass)
- Battery life under GIS load: 6.5 hours
- Battery design: sealed single cell, not user-serviceable
- Operating-temp floor: -25 C       (R-13 pass)
- Rating: MIL-STD-810H              (R-14 pass)
