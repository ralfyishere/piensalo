# Route-Consolidation Pilot — Field Report (Q3 2024)

The 2024 route-consolidation pilot ran across **12 depots** from March
through September. Consolidated routing software replaced manual dispatch at
all sites.

**Headline result: average delivery time fell by 18%** versus the 2023
baseline, measured over the Q3 window.

Operational notes:
- Driver overtime fell at 9 of 12 depots.
- Fuel spend per parcel fell 7% network-wide.
- Three depots (Nordfeld, Aviles, Kirkhaven) also changed delivery carriers
  during the pilot period.
- The steering committee recommends network-wide rollout in 2025.

Measurement details are in the accompanying methods appendix.
