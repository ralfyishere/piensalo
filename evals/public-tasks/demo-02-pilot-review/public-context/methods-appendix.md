# Methods Appendix — Route-Consolidation Pilot

Measurement window: Q3 2024, versus Q3 2023 baseline, same parcel mix.

## Correction to the headline figure (January 2025)

The field report's 18% delivery-time reduction **double-counted the
improvements at the three depots that switched carriers mid-pilot**
(Nordfeld, Aviles, Kirkhaven). Their gains cannot be attributed to route
consolidation.

**Excluding those depots, the verified reduction is 11%.**

This appendix supersedes the field report's headline number for any external
claim. The 11% figure is the one supported by the measurement design.

## Other notes
- Delivery-time data are depot-reported daily medians.
- Fuel and overtime figures are unaffected by the correction.
