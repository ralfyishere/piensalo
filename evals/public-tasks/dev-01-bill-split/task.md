# Task: Split a restaurant bill

Four friends share a restaurant bill. The food and drink subtotal is
**$84.00**. Add an **18% tip** on the subtotal, then split the grand total
**evenly four ways**. Round to cents.

Deliver, in your answer file:

1. A short `WORKING:` section showing the tip amount and the grand total.
2. A line exactly of the form `TIP: <tip amount, two decimals>`
3. A line exactly of the form `TOTAL: <grand total, two decimals>`
4. A line exactly of the form `PER_PERSON: <each person's share, two
   decimals>`

Amounts with two decimals (leading $ optional). The three summary lines are
parsed by a script; get the formats exact.
