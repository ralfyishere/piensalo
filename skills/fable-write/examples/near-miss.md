# Near-miss: the skill should NOT fire

**Task:** "Reply to my coworker that Thursday works for the demo."

A one-sentence, zero-stakes message with a single fact the requester just supplied. There is nothing to ledger, no constraint checklist, no audience model beyond the obvious.

**Why firing would hurt:** claim ledgers, structural alternatives, draft divergence, and four critic passes on a calendar confirmation turn a 10-second reply into a multi-minute production — pure latency, and the process formality risks a stiffer message than the situation calls for.
