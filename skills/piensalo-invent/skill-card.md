# Skill card: piensalo-invent

## What it does
Produces mechanism designs that survive quantitative feasibility attack, with prior-art positioning and honest novelty claims. Output is a design dossier: function tree, questioned-constraints ledger, mechanism pool, prior-art report with auditable coverage, feasibility attack outcomes, and a final design with a novelty delta per element.

## Trigger
'invent', 'design a mechanism', 'is this feasible', 'novel approach' — designing devices/mechanisms/protocols, novelty-sensitive proposals, and feasibility reviews of inventive claims.

## Counterindications
- Standard engineering selection tasks where a known component obviously fits — the divergence phase adds latency without adding design quality.
- Prior-art search is only as good as available search tools; offline it relies on model knowledge and must carry an explicit caveat.
- Not for pure build/execution tasks with a settled design.

## Negative-transfer risk
Forcing 8-15 different-principle candidates onto a solved problem yields exotic redesigns of things that work — novelty theater. The novelty-preserving synthesis step, misapplied, can also protect an unusual candidate that deserved to die on the numbers; feasibility attacks are the counterweight and must not be skipped.
