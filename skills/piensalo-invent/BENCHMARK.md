# Benchmark status: piensalo-invent

Evidence ladder (a skill may only claim the highest rung it has evidence for):

- **DESIGNED** - authored and internally reviewed; never executed as a packaged skill.
- **SMOKE_TESTED** - loaded and executed end-to-end at least once; no quality claim.
- **EXPERIMENTALLY_TESTED** - run against its eval plan with graded outcomes recorded.
- **REPLICATED** - the measured effect reproduced in an independent run.
- **PROMOTED** - controlled A/B against a no-skill baseline shows a lift; recommended for default use.
- **NARROW** - shows a lift only in a bounded niche; use inside that niche only.
- **REJECTED** - tested and found not to help (or to hurt); kept for the record.
