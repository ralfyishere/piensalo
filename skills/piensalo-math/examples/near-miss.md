# Near-miss: when piensalo-math should NOT fire

**Task:** "What is 15% of 240?"

One route, one operation, instantly checkable: 36. There is no conjecture to attack, no boundary structure, no units to validate beyond the obvious.

Why firing would hurt: demanding two independent derivations, an agreement matrix, and a symbol table for a one-line percentage adds latency and buries the answer in scaffolding - dilution with zero verification gain.
