# Benchmark status: fable-build

Status ladder (a skill may only claim the highest rung it has evidence for):

- **DESIGNED** - authored and internally linted; never executed as a packaged skill. No performance claim.
- **SMOKE_TESTED** - loaded and executed end-to-end at least once; no quality claim.
- **EXPERIMENTALLY_TESTED** - run against its eval task classes with graded outcomes recorded.
- **REPLICATED** - the experimental result reproduced in an independent run.
- **PROMOTED** - replicated lift over a no-skill baseline; recommended for general use.
- **NARROW** - tested with a real but confounded, run-specific, or population-specific result; not a general claim.
- **REJECTED** - tested and found not to help (or to hurt); kept for the record.

**Current status: DESIGNED**

The micro-skills this domain skill composes carry their own evidence levels (see the micro-skills layer); the composed domain skill itself has not been executed as a packaged unit. No performance claim of any kind is made at this rung.
